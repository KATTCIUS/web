Capy Notes

Capy Notes is a notes and daily emotion tracking application that can also help prevent procrastination through timers and music. Together with Capy, a friendly capybara, users can earn daily coins and unlock note styles, furniture, plants, and colors to decorate their space. The application is available on Google Play and the App Store for free.
