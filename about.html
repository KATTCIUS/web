About

Hello, I’m Juan Bustos, a digital artist and programmer from Córdoba, Argentina.

In recent years, I’ve focused on something I truly love: video game design and development. In 2024, I released Capy Notes, and in 2025, I published Michi Café.

I’m fascinated by stories, whether in video games, films, or animation. Through game development, I try to bring the worlds I imagine to life so that others can explore them :)
