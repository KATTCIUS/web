Projects and Experience

Capy Notes

I started developing this app in 2023 and launched it for free on iOS and Android. It is designed as a tool to organize tasks and boost productivity (To-Do lists, Pomodoro technique, Mood Tracker), all within a simple and warm interface.

What makes it unique is its gamified layer: users can water and harvest plants to upgrade their virtual home and unlock emotes for Capy, the main character, blending utility with play. The app has already surpassed 50,000 downloads on Google Play.

Character DesignCapy is a capybara and the main protagonist.
Item Design (Decor)Players can buy decorative items to customize Capy's house.
Item Design (Plants)Plants can be acquired to decorate and watered to earn coins.
Capy AnimationsCharacter animations for different situations.

Role: Solo Developer (End-to-End)
Responsible for the entire development cycle, from visual design to architecture:

  • Core Programming (C#): Developed all internal logic, including the note manager, timing systems (Pomodoro), monetization integration (Ads), and cloud save features.
  • Data Architecture: Designed scalable systems to manage inventories, user data persistence, and game states.
  • Art & Design: Created the full visual identity of the app, including character design (Capy), decorative items, plant systems, UI assets, and overall cozy aesthetic direction.
  • UX/UI & Animation: Designed full screen layouts and navigation flows. Implemented animations to enrich the user experience and bring "Capy" to life.
  • Tech Stack: Unity, C#, Android/iOS.

Michi Café

A mobile game designed to create a cozy, narrative, and entertaining experience. The player steps into the role of a cat barista managing their own coffee shop: preparing drinks, serving sweets, and attending to customers with personal stories. As the game progresses, the player can upgrade the shop, unlock characters, and customize the space.

Role: Solo Developer (End-to-End)
Comprehensive work across all areas of the project to create an immersive experience:

  • Game Systems: Implemented mechanics for crafting (drink preparation), inventory management, in-game economy, and progression logic.
  • Organic Narrative: Developed a system where customer stories unlock and reveal gradually based on service success.
  • Data Management: Robust architecture for handling orders, items, and special events, optimized for mobile devices.
  • Art & Design: Created the complete aesthetic, character design, and UI layout.
  • Tech Stack:Unity, C#, Android/iOS.
