Michi Café: Cat Café Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account data in Michi Café: Cat Café, the process is very simple. Send an email to pintordenubes.art@gmail.com, attaching the User ID that you will find in the application's settings section.

Once we receive your request, we will proceed to delete all data associated with your cloud account. This will include game progress, earned coins, obtained items, and any other information stored in our cloud service.

Our priority is to provide users with full control over their data and ensure a secure and reliable experience with Michi Café: Cat Café.
