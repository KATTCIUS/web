Michi Café: Cat Cafe

In Michi Café, you become a cute cat barista with a special mission: turn your café into the coziest and most charming place around. Enjoy preparing drinks, serving sweets, and meeting unique characters, each with their own story to discover. As you play, you’ll be able to improve your café, unlock new characters, and decorate it your way. Make your café the warmest spot in the city!
